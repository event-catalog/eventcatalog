## Overview

The **Cart System** owns the shopping cart at Acme Inc. It accepts commands to add and remove items and to check out, persists cart contents in the [[container|cart-database]], and asks the [[system|promotion-system]] to calculate the discounts that apply. When a cart is checked out it publishes a [[event|cart-checked-out]] event.
