## Overview

The **Inventory System** owns stock levels at Acme Inc. During checkout it receives [[command|reserve-inventory]] and confirms whether stock is available — replying with [[event|inventory-reserved]] or [[event|inventory-unavailable]]. It also handles [[command|release-inventory]] (when a reservation is no longer needed) and serves [[query|get-stock-level]] lookups from the [[container|inventory-database]].
