In EventCatalog you can assign teams or owners to domains, services or messages.

Teams are collection of users in EventCatalog, and they can have their own documented page.

### Teams in EventCatalog

- Teams live in the `/teams` folder.
- Teams reference users within it's frontmatter.
- Teams can own domains, services or messages.
