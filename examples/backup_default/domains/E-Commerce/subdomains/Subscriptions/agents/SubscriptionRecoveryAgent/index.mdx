The Subscription Recovery Agent supports recurring revenue operations by recommending the next best recovery action when payment collection fails.

It receives [[event|SubscriptionPaymentDue]], [[event|PaymentFailed]], and [[event|UserSubscriptionCancelled]] so billing and support teams can keep the customer active when recovery is still likely.

<AgentTools />

## Responsibilities

- Recommend retry timing and grace-period actions for failed renewals.
- Explain entitlement impact before a subscription is suspended.
- Draft customer-facing payment recovery messages for the notification queue.

## Collaborators

The agent works with [[service|BillingService]], [[service|SubscriptionService]], [[service|PaymentGatewayService]], and [[service|NotificationService]] during the [[flow|SubscriptionPaymentFailureFlow]].
