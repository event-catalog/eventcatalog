## Overview

The **Warehouse** entity represents a physical distribution center or storage facility. It acts as the location dimension for all stock items and is referenced by stock movements for transfer tracking.

## Relationships

* **StockItem:** A warehouse holds many stock items, one per SKU
* **StockMovement:** Movements reference a warehouse as source or destination

## Business Rules

* A warehouse must be active to receive inbound stock
* Deactivating a warehouse requires transferring all stock items first
* `currentUtilization` cannot exceed `capacity`
* Warehouse codes must be globally unique
