## Overview

A **Reservation** represents a temporary claim on inventory by an order. It ensures that stock is not double-sold between order confirmation and physical shipment.

Reservations are always created through the `StockItem` aggregate root to enforce the invariant that reserved quantity never exceeds available quantity.

## Lifecycle

1. **PENDING** — Created when `OrderConfirmed` is received
2. **CONFIRMED** — Validated by the stock item aggregate
3. **FULFILLED** — Stock physically picked and shipped (creates a `StockMovement`)
4. **CANCELLED** — Order cancelled, reserved units released back
5. **EXPIRED** — TTL exceeded, units automatically released

## Business Rules

* A reservation must reference an existing, active `StockItem`
* Reservations expire after a configurable TTL (default 30 minutes)
* Fulfilling a reservation decrements `quantityOnHand` and creates a `StockMovement`
* Cancelling or expiring a reservation decrements `quantityReserved`
