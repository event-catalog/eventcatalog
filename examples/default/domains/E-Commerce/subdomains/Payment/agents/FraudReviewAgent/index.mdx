The Fraud Review Agent supports the [[domain|Payment]] domain by reviewing risky payment attempts before they continue through the payment gateway.

It receives [[event|PaymentInitiated]], [[event|RiskScoreCalculated]], and [[event|FraudDetected]] from payment and fraud detection services. It uses risk profile tooling to explain why a payment was flagged and recommend whether payment processing should continue.

## Responsibilities

- Explain fraud signals in plain language for risk analysts.
- Recommend approve, decline, or manual review decisions.
- Keep the fraud case queue up to date with payment risk context.

## Collaborators

The agent works with [[service|FraudDetectionService]], [[service|PaymentService]], and [[service|PaymentGatewayService]] during the [[flow|PaymentFlow]].
