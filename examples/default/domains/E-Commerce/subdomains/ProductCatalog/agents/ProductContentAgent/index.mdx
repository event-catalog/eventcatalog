The Product Content Agent helps the [[domain|ProductCatalog]] domain keep customer-facing product pages useful as inventory and merchandising data changes.

It receives [[event|InventoryGenerated]] and [[event|InventoryAdjusted]] so content teams can prioritize high-demand or newly stocked products for enrichment.

## Responsibilities

- Normalize supplier and inventory attributes into catalog-friendly product facts.
- Draft merchandising copy from approved product data instead of free-form guesses.
- Summarize review themes for merchandising and support teams.

## Collaborators

The agent works with [[service|InventoryService]] and supports product availability decisions surfaced during the [[flow|InventoryReservationFlow]].
