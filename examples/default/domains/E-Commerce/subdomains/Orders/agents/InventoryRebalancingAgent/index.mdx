The Inventory Rebalancing Agent supports the [[domain|Orders]] domain when the catalog has demand but the warehouse network does not have stock in the right place.

It receives [[event|OutOfStock]] and [[event|InventoryAdjusted]] so it can recommend whether operations should transfer stock, split a shipment, backorder the order, or notify the customer.

## Responsibilities

- Explain why a SKU is constrained and which warehouse is causing the bottleneck.
- Recommend transfer, backorder, substitution, or cancellation actions.
- Give operations teams a short rationale that can be attached to the order case.

## Collaborators

The agent works with [[service|InventoryService]], [[service|OrdersService]], and [[service|NotificationService]] during the [[flow|InventoryReservationFlow]].
