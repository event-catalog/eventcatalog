## Overview

The **Shipping System** connects Acme Inc to its carriers. When the [[system|warehouse-system]] publishes [[event|order-ready-for-shipping]], the [[service|shipping-api]] selects a carrier and the [[service|carrier-adapter]] sends a [[command|create-shipment]] to the external [[system|carrier]] to dispatch the parcel.
