## What are ADRs?

Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) are documents that capture important architectural decisions made along with their context and consequences.

## How to use this section

- **Published ADRs** - Approved and implemented decisions
- **Draft ADRs** - Decisions under review
- **Examples** - Template and example ADRs for reference

## Creating a new ADR

When creating a new ADR, follow our standard template format and place it in the `drafts` folder until it's approved.
