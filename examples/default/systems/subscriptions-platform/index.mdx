## Overview

The **Subscriptions Platform** groups the services that manage recurring revenue.
`SubscriptionService` owns the lifecycle of a subscription (start, renew, cancel)
while `BillingService` turns billing cycles into invoices and schedules payments.

It is orthogonal to the domains those services live in — `SubscriptionService` and
`BillingService` both sit in the **Subscriptions** subdomain, but the system view
lets you reason about recurring billing as one capability.
