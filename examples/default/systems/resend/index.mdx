## Overview

**Resend** is a third-party email delivery platform. It sits outside our
organisation — we integrate with it to actually deliver the transactional
emails our **Notifications System** composes.

Because it is an external system, it is shaded in the Context Diagram so it's
clear at a glance that it is not something we own or operate.
